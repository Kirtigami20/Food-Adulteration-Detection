Ensuring Public Health through IoT and AI Vision
FoodSensor is an end-to-end IoT solution designed to empower individuals to detect and prevent the consumption of adulterated food. By combining real-time hardware sensors with a machine-learning-powered web dashboard, the system identifies harmful substances and assesses food quality in everyday items like milk, water, fruits, and vegetables.
